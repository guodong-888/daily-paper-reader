## 摘要
急性应激会触发快速的生理适应，然而不同应激模式如何塑造人体循环分子景观仍不清楚。我们通过三种范式（心理应激、可控身体应激以及蹦极引起的心理-身体复合应激）进行了纵向深度血浆蛋白质组学与靶向代谢组学分析。尽管下丘脑-垂体-肾上腺轴被强烈激活，心理应激仅引起极小的血浆蛋白质组和代谢变化，而身体应激则触发快速、协调的蛋白质组重塑，并伴随应激源特异性的代谢适应。复合应激引发最强且最持久的蛋白质组反应，定义了跨应激模式的梯度分子特征。跨范式整合识别出一个应激相关蛋白核心，富含免疫颗粒效应物、RNA结合蛋白及囊泡相关特征，提示存在调控性胞外释放。在混合应激范式中，胞外囊泡谱分析支持囊泡相关分泌，而功能实验表明单独的糖皮质激素信号不足以触发释放。这些发现共同揭示了应激源特性是人类循环分子反应的关键决定因素。

## Abstract
Acute stress triggers rapid physiological adaptations, yet how distinct stress modalities shape the human circulating molecular landscape remains unclear. We performed longitudinal deep plasma proteomics and targeted metabolomics across three paradigms: psychological stress, controlled physical stress, and combined psychological-physical stress induced by bungee jumping. Despite robust hypothalamic-pituitary-adrenal axis activation, psychological stress induced minimal plasma proteomic and metabolic changes, whereas physical stress triggered rapid, coordinated proteome remodeling accompanied by stressor-specific metabolic adaptations. Combined stress elicited the strongest and most persistent proteomic response, defining a graded molecular signature across stress modalities. Cross-paradigm integration identified a stress-associated protein core enriched for immune granule effectors, RNA-binding proteins, and vesicle-linked signatures, suggesting regulated extracellular release. Consistently, extracellular vesicle profiling in the mixed-stress paradigm supported vesicle-associated secretion, while functional experiments indicated that glucocorticoid signaling alone is insufficient to trigger release. Together, these findings reveal stressor identity as a key determinant of the human circulating molecular response.
