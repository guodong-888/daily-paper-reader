## 摘要
骨骼肌和血管持续暴露于机械力，特别是在运动过程中。我们对人内皮细胞和骨骼肌细胞施加周期性机械拉伸以模拟运动，并研究了急性分子响应。机械加载引起了转录组和代谢组谱中共享的和细胞类型特异性的改变，其中一些变化与运动后在体内观察到的变化相似。两种细胞类型在响应机械加载时都释放乙酸，至少部分是通过活性氧依赖的机制。有趣的是，内皮细胞和肌肉细胞中的转录组变化方向相反。例如，与电子传递链相关的基因在内皮细胞中被抑制，但在骨骼肌细胞中被上调。在内皮细胞中，机械加载重塑了细胞间连接，促进了指示屏障完整性增加和增殖减弱的转录组转变。代谢变化在内皮细胞中更为显著，表现为葡萄糖衍生的丝氨酸生物合成增加，如13C-(U)-葡萄糖示踪所示。靶向丝氨酸合成途径中的关键酶磷酸甘油酸脱氢酶（PHGDH）强调了丝氨酸生物合成在内皮细胞合成代谢中的作用。这些发现表明，机械加载重现了内皮细胞和肌肉细胞中几种运动诱导的效应，并揭示了机械刺激、丝氨酸合成与内皮细胞静止之间的潜在联系。

## Abstract
Skeletal muscles and blood vessels are continuously exposed to mechanical forces, particularly during exercise. We subjected human endothelial and skeletal muscle cells to cyclic mechanical stretch to mimic exercise and investigated acute molecular responses. Mechanical loading elicited both shared and cell type-specific alterations in transcriptomic and metabolomic profiles, several of which mirrored changes observed in vivo following exercise. Both cell types released acetate in response to mechanical loading, at least partly via reactive oxygen species -dependent mechanism. Interestingly, transcriptomic changes occurred in opposite directions in endothelial and muscle cells. For example, genes associated with the electron transport chain were repressed in endothelial cells but upregulated in skeletal muscle cells. In endothelial cells mechanical loading remodelled intercellular junctions, promoted a transcriptomic shift indicative of increased barrier integrity and attenuated proliferation. Metabolic changes were more pronounced in endothelial cells, which exhibited increased serine biosynthesis from glucose, as demonstrated by 13C-(U)-glucose tracing. Targeting phosphoglycerate dehydrogenase (PHGDH), a key enzyme in the serine synthesis pathway, underscored the role of serine biosynthesis in endothelial cell anabolism. These findings suggest that mechanical loading recapitulates several exercise-induced effects in endothelial and muscle cells, and highlights a potential link between mechanical stimuli, serine synthesis, and endothelial cell quiescence.
