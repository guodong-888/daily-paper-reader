## 摘要
骨骼肌稳态依赖于蛋白质周转和线粒体质量控制的协调调控；然而，连接这些过程的分子机制仍不清楚。在本研究中，我们利用组成型LAP3缺陷小鼠，研究了亮氨酸氨肽酶3（LAP3）——一种后蛋白水解氨肽酶的生理作用。LAP3缺陷优先影响骨骼肌，导致两性小鼠肌肉质量减少和线粒体增大。雌性LAP3缺陷小鼠还表现出肌纤维尺寸减小、耐力能力受损、能量消耗增加、脂质氧化升高以及线粒体附近脂滴积累。蛋白质组学分析揭示了与脂质代谢和蛋白质稳态相关的通路重塑。与这些发现一致，LAP3缺陷增加了Pink1和Tax1bp1的表达，并促进了泛素化蛋白的积累，提示线粒体质量控制和蛋白质稳态调控的改变。在培养的肌源性细胞中，LAP3定位于线粒体组分，LAP3敲低和过表达均改变线粒体形态。综上所述，这些结果确定LAP3是骨骼肌稳态的调节因子，并支持LAP3在连接细胞内肽周转与线粒体稳态中的作用，其中雌性骨骼肌对LAP3缺陷表现出更大的易感性。

## Abstract
Skeletal muscle homeostasis depends on the coordinated regulation of protein turnover and mitochondrial quality control; however, the molecular mechanisms linking these processes remain unclear. In this study, we examined the physiological role of leucine aminopeptidase 3 (LAP3), a post-proteolytic aminopeptidase, using constitutive LAP3-deficient mice. LAP3 deficiency preferentially affected skeletal muscle, causing reduced muscle mass and mitochondrial enlargement in both sexes. Female LAP3-deficient mice also showed reduced myofiber size, impaired endurance capacity, increased energy expenditure, elevated lipid oxidation, and lipid droplet accumulation adjacent to the mitochondria. Proteomic analyses revealed remodeling of pathways related to lipid metabolism and protein homeostasis. Consistent with these findings, LAP3 deficiency increased the expression of Pink1 and Tax1bp1 and promoted the accumulation of ubiquitinated proteins, suggesting alterations in mitochondrial quality control and proteostatic regulation. In cultured myogenic cells, LAP3 localized to mitochondrial fractions, and both LAP3 knockdown and overexpression altered mitochondrial morphology. Taken together, these results identify LAP3 as a regulator of skeletal muscle homeostasis and support a role for LAP3 in linking intracellular peptide turnover to mitochondrial homeostasis, with female skeletal muscle showing greater susceptibility to LAP3 deficiency.
